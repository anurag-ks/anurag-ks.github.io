About Us

Paradigm is a professional hub for 3D printing services. We provide fast and cost effective 3D printed products using state of the art technologies. Our objective is to offer the best quality products in plastics at rapid speed. Founded in 2019, Paradigm is fuelled by the determination to advance the current technological arena and simplify the complexities of 3D printing for maximum people to integrate its benefits into their workflow.

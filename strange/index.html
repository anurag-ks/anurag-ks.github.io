About Us

We are a small group of engineering students who aim to set forth into the product design industry with a novel manufacturing and rapid prototyping product. Based out of Manipal, the team was formed to participate in a State level B-plan competition conducted by MUTBI, an incubator supported by the National Science & Technology Entrepreneurship Development Board (NSTEDB). Strange Matter aims to build technology that will be accessible to a wide array of designers, engineers, academicians, hardware manufacturers and PCB manufacturing units to name a few. Strange Matter was the winner of Provenance 2015 under the name Team Modulus. We hope to play a significant role in the gradual advent of the maker movement and the rapid prototyping and precision machining era in India.

Our Team

Our Goal

Manifest our idea of developing a CNC Mill; accessible and easy to use, which will help the field of CNC machining venture into a vast number of domains that it hasn’t yet taken off into. We endeavor to cast aside barriers and conventions that have prohibited the entry of the product into otherwise untapped high potential fields.

The integration of an extensive Software Application which will provide great mobility, incorporate ease of use and support various input files for machining is on our agenda. All this, tightly coupled with the elimination of G-Codes, promises to deliver a product different from the rest of its counterparts. Once the product is out on the line, an extensive support platform will be set up.

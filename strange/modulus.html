Modulus

The Technology

Post inception Strange Matter’s flagship product, a desktop CNC is now under a comprehensive design and market analysis phase. The product will incorporate the most reliable tested and successful mechanical designs while adding a tinge of extra flavor aimed at taking it a step further from existing technology. The compact, affordable CNC will provide maximum functionality, great power and impeccable precision for its grade. Variable material machining and customization are being given the hallmark status during the process of Design Conception.

On the Software front, as visualized, progress continues to be made towards building an extensible, user friendly interface that will eliminate the use of G-Codes to simplify the process. Cross platform accessibility and a versatile software support base continue to be on the progress agenda. Special attention is being directed towards integrating sophisticated operations into a user-friendly environment.

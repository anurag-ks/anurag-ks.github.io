A little about me

I am an Engineer and a Maker, who is passionate about automating things.The field of Robotics and Automation has seen a exponential growth in the past decade and will expand its reach even more in the future. Most of my work today is centered around digital fabrication and I would love to work on projects related to robotics and automation.

My team and I started Strange Matter with the vision of making CNC fabrication affordable and simpler. Strange Matter had been selected among the top 30 teams in India in the "Texas Instruments India Innovation Challenge 2016". We are currently working on making "Modulus 1" a reality.
